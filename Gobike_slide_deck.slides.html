Investigation Overview¶

My goal for this presentation is to introduce my findings of Gobike dataset with you, and shere my excitement of the power of visualizations.

Dataset Overview¶

I used Ford Gobike Tripdata(2017) available on https://s3.amazonaws.com/baywheels-data/index.html \ The dataset contains 519,700 rows of observations of:

1.How long customers use Gobike service,

2.Where they start, and finish their Gobike experiences,

3.User type (customer or subscriber)

I also added columns to show:

1.What day customers use Gobike service,

2.Distance of each Gobike travels

Univariate Exploration¶

I made some visualization of single columns to see their characterestics.

I tried to find trends of;

1.Driving duration

2.Travel distance

3.Difference between days

4.Difference between weekdays and weekends

5.difference between user types

1.Driving Duration¶

Most Gobikers use service for a short time, probably around 1000 seconds(16min).

Multivariate Exploration¶

Let's explore the database deeper to see how the three categorical measures play into the relationship between duration and distance.

The plot on the left describes subscriber spend much less time than customer. Also, the plot on the right indicates travel distances are longer on Monday to Friday for both customer and subscriber because they use Gobike for commute. Now it is obvious that subscriber know where they want to go, and they are using Gobike service to commute specific locations(stations, companies or schools).

                  dodge = 0.3, linestyles = "")

plt.ylabel('distance', fontsize = 10)
plt.title('Distance in a week; Customers vs. subscribers', fontsize = 15);

By doing first multivariate exploration(Bar chart), I am pretty sure subscriber use Gobike for commute because they spend much less time than customers on any day. That means subscribers know where they are going. And second chart(Line plot) strengthened my guessing even more, it shows travel distance for both customers and subscrivers are higher on weekdays. This trend clearly be seen especially on subscriber side.
